L01Interest Rates and Discount Rates 利率与贴现率

基础(1a P1)

\(i_t=\frac{a(t)-a(t-1)}{a(t-1)}\),\(a(0)=1\),\(A(t)=k\,a(t)\)

单利 \(a=1+it\)(\(i_t\) 递减)· 复利 \(a=(1+i)^t\)

\(v=\frac{1}{1+i}\),\(PV=v^t\)

贴现率(1a P2)

\(d_t=\frac{a(t)-a(t-1)}{a(t)}\)(年末基)

推\(1-d=v\) · \(d=iv\) · \(i=\frac{d}{1-d}\) · \(i-d=id\) · \(\frac1d-\frac1i=1\)

\(PV=(1-d)^t\),\(AV=(1-d)^{-t}\)

名义利率

推\((1+\frac{i^{(m)}}{m})^m=1+i\) · \((1-\frac{d^{(m)}}{m})^m=1-d\)

\(i^{(p)}=p[(1+\frac{i^{(m)}}{m})^{m/p}-1]\)

期长由名义率描述决定(m 可 = 1/2)

利息力(1b–1f)

\(\delta_t=\frac{a'}{a}=\frac{d}{dt}\ln a(t)\)

推\(a(t)=e^{\int_0^t\delta}\);恒定:\(e^\delta=1+i\),\(\delta=\ln(1+i)\)

\(i^{(\infty)}=d^{(\infty)}=\delta\)(连续复利)

变力陷阱(1g)

推\(AV=\frac{a(t_2)}{a(t_1)}=e^{\int_{t_1}^{t_2}\delta}\)

\(PV=e^{-\int_{t_1}^{t_2}\delta}\)

simple interest:从存入日重启 \(1+it\)

等价利率(1h)+ 级数(1f)

推\(1+i=(1+\frac{i^{(m)}}{m})^m=(1-\frac{d^{(m)}}{m})^{-m}=e^\delta=\frac1v\)

\(i=d+d^2+\cdots\) · \(d=i-i^2+\cdots\) · \(\delta=i-\frac{i^2}{2}+\frac{i^3}{3}-\cdots\)

变利率下等价必须指明时段

L08Duration, Convexity, and Immunization 久期、凸性与免疫

久期

推\(D_{mac}=\frac{\sum t v^t A_t}{P}\) · \(D_{mod}=vD_{mac}\)

零息=n · 永续=\(\frac{1+i}{i}\) · 平价债=\(\ddot a_{\overline{n}|}\) · 增长股=\(\frac{1+i}{i-g}\)

一阶近似

推修正:\(P\approx P_0[1-\Delta i\,D_{mod}]\)(恒低估)

Macaulay:\(P\approx P_0(\frac{1+i_0}{1+i})^{D_{mac}}\)(更准)

组合与时间

组合久期 = 价格加权平均

随时间锯齿下降,付息瞬间跳升

凸性

\(C_{mod}=\frac{\sum t(t+1)v^{t+2}A_t}{P}\) · \(C_{mac}=\frac{\sum t^2v^tA_t}{P}\)

\(C_{mod}=\frac{C_{mac}+D_{mac}}{(1+i)^2}\)

Redington

\(P_A=P_L\) · \(P'_A=P'_L\) · \(P''_A\gt P''_L\)

= 盈余在 i₀ 相对极小;第三条件 ⇔ t² 加权比较

Full / Dedication

L09Spot Rates and Forward Rates 即期利率与远期利率

Spot

\(AV=(1+r_t)^t\) · \(P=\sum CF_t(1+r_t)^{-t}\)

零息债:\(r_t=P_t^{-1/t}-1\)

Forward 记号

\(f_{[t_1,t_2]}\):t₁→t₂ 年实际利率

m-yr fwd deferred t yrs = \(f_{[t,t+m]}\) · \(f_{[0,1]}=r_1\)

核心等式

推\((1+r_{t_1})^{t_1}(1+f_{[t_1,t_2]})^{t_2-t_1}=(1+r_{t_2})^{t_2}\)

幂次自查:两边总时长相等

几何平均视角

\(1+r_t=[\prod(1+f_{[j,j+1]})]^{1/t}\)

\(P=F\prod(1+f_{[j,j+1]})^{-1}\)

Yield(YTM)

使 \(\sum CF_t(1+i)^{-t}=P\) 的单一 i

TVM:N·PV·PMT·FV,CPT I/Y

锁定构造
