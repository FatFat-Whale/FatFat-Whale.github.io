L32Moment Generating Function 矩母函数

定义 / 取矩

\(M_X(t)=E[e^{tX}]\),\(M_X(0)=1\)

\(M_X^{(n)}(0)=E[X^n]\)

\(E[X]=M'(0)\),\(\mathrm{Var}=M''(0)-\big(M'(0)\big)^2\)

独立和 / 唯一性

\(X\perp Y:\ M_{X+Y}=M_XM_Y\)

MGF 唯一确定分布(认形状)

指数 \(\tfrac{1}{1-\theta t}\) · 伽马 \(\tfrac{1}{(1-\theta t)^\alpha}\)

泊松 \(e^{\lambda(e^t-1)}\) · 正态 \(e^{\mu t+\sigma^2t^2/2}\) · 二项 \((1-p+pe^t)^n\)

L33Probability Generating Function 概率母函数

定义 / 求值

\(P_X(t)=E[t^X]=\sum_k t^k p_k\)

\(P(1)=1\)(自检)

\(P(0)=p_0\);\(P[X{=}k]=\dfrac{P^{(k)}(0)}{k!}\)

限非负整数 RV

矩 / 独立和 / MGF

\(P'(1)=E[X]\),\(P''(1)=E[X(X{-}1)]\)

\(\operatorname{Var}=P''(1)+P'(1)-\big(P'(1)\big)^2\)

独立和:\(P_{X+Y}=P_X\,P_Y\)

\(P(t)=M(\ln t)\);泊松 \(P(t)=e^{\lambda(t-1)}\)

L34Transformations 变换

cdf 法(通用)

\(F_Y(y)=\Pr\big(g(X)\le y\big)\)

\(g\!\uparrow:\ F_Y(y)=F_X\big(g^{-1}(y)\big)\)

\(g\!\downarrow:\ F_Y(y)=1-F_X\big(g^{-1}(y)\big)\)

再 \(f_Y(y)=\dfrac{d}{dy}F_Y(y)\)

密度法(单调 / 分支)

单调:\(f_Y(y)=f_X\big(g^{-1}(y)\big)\,\big|\tfrac{d}{dy}g^{-1}(y)\big|\)

雅可比 \(\big|\tfrac{dx}{dy}\big|\) 取绝对值

非单调:\(f_Y(y)=\sum_i f_X(h_i(y))\,|h_i'(y)|\)

支撑随变换改 · \(x\) 全代成 \(g^{-1}(y)\)

L35Transformations of Two or More Variables 多变量变换

雅可比法

补 \(W\) → 逆变换 \(x=h_1,\ y=h_2\)

\(J=\det\left(\begin{smallmatrix}x_z&x_w\\ y_z&y_w\end{smallmatrix}\right)=x_zy_w-x_wy_z\)

\(f_{Z,W}=f_{X,Y}(h_1,h_2)\,\lvert J\rvert\)

\(f_Z(z)=\int f_{Z,W}(z,w)\,dw\)

常见变换 + 卷积

商 \(X/Y\):\(W=Y,\ x=zw,\ \lvert J\rvert=w\)

积 \(XY\):\(W=Y,\ x=z/w,\ \lvert J\rvert=1/w\)

和 \(X+Y\):\(W=Y,\ x=z-w,\ \lvert J\rvert=1\)

独立和:\(f_Z=\int f_X(z-w)f_Y(w)\,dw\)
